The Straw hat crew - Original Crew

Born from a captain’s impossible dream, the Straw Hat Pirates began as scattered souls with fragile hopes. Luffy gave each member the freedom to chase dreams they once thought unreachable. Together, they toppled tyrants, rewrote the fate of nations, and shook the world’s foundations. A family bound not by blood, but by courage and destiny.

a world where the weight of history rests in the hearts of pirates.

Wealth, fame, power... Gold Roger, the King of the Pirates, obtained this and everything else the world had to offer and his dying words drove countless souls to the seas.


"You want my treasure? You can have it! I left everything I gathered together in one place! Now you just have to find it!"



These words lured men to the Grand Line. In pursuit of dreams greater than they've ever dared to imagine! This is the time known as the 'Great Pirate Era'!
